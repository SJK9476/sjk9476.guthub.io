    About Me

    I am 28 years of age and recently began my journey into the world of software engineering. My journey began after finally realising that a life in retail management just isn't for me. The last 10 years have been spent dealing with the disorganisation and frustration of a job sector that gives you half the tools and double the expectations year on year! So I spent some time thinking about the life I wanted and the things I enjoyed. After some introspection I landed on the tech industry and it's importance to every part of life as we know it. It's been staring me in the face this whole time. I enjoy methodical work and really unraveling a problem. So I took the leap and signed up for the Dev30 course offered by the team over at Parsity. I got my teeth stuck into some Javascript and really enjoyed the experience, so my next step was to signup for CodeAcademy Pro and the rest is history. This site will be a constant work in progress as my knowledge base and skillset improves, an ongoing project for me to guage where I'm at and the areas I need to focus on, it's already becoming increasingly apparent that Google, W3School and MDN are my new best friends.
